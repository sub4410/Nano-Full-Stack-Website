Our introduction

Welcome to
Hands-On Learning Redefined

At Nano School, we harness the power of experiential learning. Our 14-day workshops, led by IIT alumni, offer students practical, hands-on experience in robotics, coding, and design. Held at prestigious institutions like IIT Delhi, NIFT Delhi, and IIM, our programs are designed to foster creativity and innovation, ensuring students apply theoretical concepts to real-world scenarios.
